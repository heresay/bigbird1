Big Bird

A character from Sesame Street .

I am a 6-year-old walking, talking canary. I have long, bright orange legs.
I am 8 feet 2 inches tall and my nest is on Sesame Street. I sleep with a teddy bear named Radar.
I am neighbors with a grouch, who takes care of a worm named Wormie.

Frequent Misunderstandings

  • I once sung the alphabet as one long word.
    I wondered what it meant afterwards.
  • I refer to the grocer Mr. Hooper as "Mr.Looper".
  • At one point none of the human adults on Sesame Street saw Snuffy,
    my best friend, they thought I made up him to stay out of trouble.

What I've Learned

I've learned to read and write while on Sesame Street.

Learning the alphabet has helped me with my reading and writing.

I recently spoke on a podcast "Foley and Friends".
We talked about life in lockdown and birdseed banana bread.
